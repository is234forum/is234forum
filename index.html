Ms. Truss:

Dear Cunningham Students, I am happy to announce that our annual Hour of Code week is next week. You will be able to participate in coding activities during your Math class, during your lunch periods in the library, and compete in a grade wide event on select days throughout the week. The Grade -Wide competition will take place beginning Monday 12/5 for grade 6, Tuesday 12/6 for grade 7, and Wednesday 12/7 for grade 8. Winners will receive Cunningham Gear, certificates, and/or trophies! In order to compete in the grade-wide competition, students are required to complete a qualifying activity. The activities are grade specific and are listed below: You must register an account on the Hour of Code website HOUROFCODE.COM before beginning the activity. If you do not register an account, the system will not know your name and you will not get credit.
